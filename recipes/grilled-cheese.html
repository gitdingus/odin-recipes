Grilled Cheese

Description

With a few simple ingredients you will be able to make an amazing grilled cheese sandwich. The only ingredients you need are bread, cheese, and butter.

I recommend cooking your grilled cheese in a cast iron skillet with a lid. It helps to have an egg timer close by so you don't burn your bread!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Canadian White Bread
  • 4 slices Boarshead Vermont Yellow Cheddar
  • 1 TBSP Salted Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the skillet over a medium-low heat
  2. Add 1/2 TBSP of butter to the pan and let it melt evenly over the surface
  3. Lower heat
  4. Put 4 slices of cheese in between your two slices of bread
  5. Place your sandwich on the skillet and cover with lid and set timer for 2 minutes
  6. When your two minutes are up take the sandwich off the skillet and melt the other 1/2 TBSP of butter on skillet
  7. Place your sandwich back in the skillet, covering with lid, on the uncooked side for two minutes
  8. Flip sandwich every two minutes until total cook time is eight minutes or bread is golden-brown and cheese is melted
  9. Enjoy!
