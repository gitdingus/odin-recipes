Mac and Cheese with Hotdogs

Description

This is a simple but tasty dish. All you need are some hotdogs and a box of Kraft Mac and Cheese for an easy meal that is sure to satisfy your belly! For some extra kick add some Texas Petes hot sauce... Yum!

Ingredients

  • 1 box Kraft Mac and Cheese
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 5 TBSP of butter
  • 2 hotdogs

Steps

  1. Follow the recipe on the Kraft Mac and Cheese box using the 1/4 cup of milk and 4 TBSP of butter
  2. Thinly slice two hotdogs
  3. Melt 1 TBSP of butter in pan over medium heat
  4. Add hotdog slices to pan making sure they are in one layer
  5. Be sure to brown both sides of the hotdog slices
  6. Once cooked, add hotdog slices to bowl of Mac and Cheese and mix it up
  7. Add hot sauce to taste
  8. Enjoy!
